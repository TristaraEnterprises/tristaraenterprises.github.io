Graduate Student

If you have already finished a four-year undergraduate degree, commonly a BS or BA degree, you can apply for graduate school in the United States. Although most people with a BA or BS apply for a masters program, it is possible to apply directly to a Ph.D. program in some cases, or to a joint masters-Ph.D. program in others.

The length of graduate programs varies depending on the subject and the school. To get a teaching degree for public education in the United States, for example, typical masters programs are about one year long. Many other masters programs are two years long or more. Most Ph.D. programs are at least three years long, and some can be much longer, depending on research and practice requirements.
