Language School Application Process

Language school applications generally do not require as much information as do university applications. There is often an online application form, which will include information about the dates you want to study, your housing preferences, and the type of course(s) you are interested in. After you have applied and sent proof of your financial support or, in some cases, a deposit or full payment for the program, the language school sends you an I-20 form that you can use to apply for your visa.
