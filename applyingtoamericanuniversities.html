Applying to American Universities

  • New Freshman

    • You are a new freshman if you have never attended college before. Some universities also consider you to be a new freshman if you have not completed one year of college yet.

  • Transfer Student

    • You are a transfer student if you have already completed a minimum number of college credits. The number of required credits varies by school.

  • Graduate Student

    • You are a graduate student if you have already completed a BA or BS (or if you will have completed it by the time you begin study on your master's or Ph.D. program).

  • English Language Institute

    • You can attend an institute just for studying English. These institutes may be parts of universities or they may be independent schools.
