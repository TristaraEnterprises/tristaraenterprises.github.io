Our Fees

Applying to study in a foreign country can be expensive, but it doesn't have to cost a fortune. Since our company is run by both Americans with graduate level degrees and university teaching experience and Chinese with graduate level study experience in America, we know the process well. We charge one flat fee for our basic service, whether you are applying as a new college freshman, a transfer student, a graduate student, or whether you want to attend a language institute.

Our fee: $2,800 per student. (That's just 16,000 RMB.)

How can our fee be so much less than most college recruitment agency chains in China? We work hard to keep costs down. For one thing, we don't have hundreds of offices that we have to maintain. We are a modern company that saves costs by operating mostly online, using Skype and email as our main forms of communication with clients. In addition, we don't have a large corporate structure of managers and owners who take a salary but don't actually assist you much in applying for schools. Finally, we know that the real price of applying for a university in America simply doesn't cost that much, and our goal is to help you find a school in an affordable manner.

Other Fees: In addition to our basic package, we offer extra services for an extra fee.

  • Applications to More Than Five Schools: $200 per Extra School, Plus Application Fees
  • Assistance Registering for Classes for the First Semester: $200
