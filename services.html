Our Services

We offer several services to students applying to schools in America to walk you through the entire process. All of the following are included in our basic package at no extra charge.

  • Skype Consultation
  • Detailed Outline of the Application Process
  • Application Paperwork for Up to Five Schools
  • Paperwork for Getting Transfer Credits
  • Application Essay
  • Practice for Any Required Interviews via Skype
  • Detailed Email Updates Every Two Days

In addition, we also offer optional extra services for a fee.

  • Application Paperwork for More Than Five Schools
  • Registering for Classes
