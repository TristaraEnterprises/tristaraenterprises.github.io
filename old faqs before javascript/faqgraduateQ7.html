Graduate Student Frequently Asked Questions

Question:

Should I contact professors before applying? How?

Answer:

If you have questions about the program or the specific research a professor is conducting, or if you want to ask a professor to consider being your advisor, you can contact them before or while applying. Usually students contact professors by email.
