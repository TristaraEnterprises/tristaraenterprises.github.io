Question:

Why do you charge extra for extra services? Why don't you include everything in the base fee?

Answer:

Not every student requires every service. In order to keep your cost as low as possible, we don't want to charge people for a service they don't need. Therefore, we charge our basic fee for all of the necessary services that everyone should use. If anyone wants extra services, they can pay for those services separately, so that others who don't want or need those services don't have to pay for them.
