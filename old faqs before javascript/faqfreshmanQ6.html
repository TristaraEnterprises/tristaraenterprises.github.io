Freshman Frequently Asked Questions

Question:

When should I start the application process?

Answer:

American high school students begin applying for universities about one year before they plan to begin classes. It is most common for students to begin college in the fall. Most deadlines for applications are in the early spring. Since international applicants need to prepare more paperwork, it is important for you to begin your application as early as possible, at least a year before you want to begin classes.
