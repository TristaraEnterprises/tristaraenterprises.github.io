Transfer Student Frequently Asked Questions

Question:

Where will I live?

Answer:

Most university freshmen and sophomores in the United States live in dormitories on campus. Some universities even require freshmen and sophomores to live on campus.

For schools that have no on-campus housing requirements, it may be possible to find an apartment or a host family to stay with, but these arrangements require more planning and finances. If you want to live off campus, let us know as soon as possible so that we can find universities that allow it if you will be transfering as a freshman or sophomore.
