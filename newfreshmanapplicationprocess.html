New Freshman Application Process

American students begin applying to universities one year before they plan to enter college. For example, if you plan to enter college in the fall of 2016, you would begin the application process in the fall of 2015. For international students, it is important to begin the application process at least this early, and possibly earlier.

In general, freshmen need to complete an online application and submit an application fee. They must also take a standard test such as the SAT or ACT. International students also need to take an English test, usually the TOEFL. You need to submit your high school transcript(s), recommendation letters from teachers or other adults who know you well (not family), and a record of your extra-curricular activities such as sports, clubs, volunteer activities, work experience, competitions, or other interesting information that can set you apart from other students. You may need to write an essay or have a phone or online interview. Finally, international students need to submit bank statements proving their family can pay for tuition.
