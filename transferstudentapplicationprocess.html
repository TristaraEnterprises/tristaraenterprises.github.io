Transfer Student Application Process

Some universities allow you to transfer only in the fall, while some allow spring transfers as well. If the university you want to attend has quarters instead of semesters, you may be able to transfer into any quarter: spring, summer, winter, or fall.

In general, transfer students need to submit an online application. You also need transcripts, a TOEFL score, letters of recommendation, and a bank statement from your family showing you can afford to pay for tuition. Depending on the school or program you want to enter, you may need to write an essay, submit a portfolio, or have an interview.
